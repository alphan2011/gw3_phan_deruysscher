Slytherin

"Or perhaps in Slytherin,

You'll make your real friends,

Those cunning folk use any means,

To achieve their ends"

What will you miss most about Hogwarts?

Draco Malfoy

Being around lots of other kids my age. I’ll no longer have as many opportunities to bully my inferiors. Especially Potter and his gang. (His father will be hearing about this.)

Vincent Crabbe

The food. Corned beef sandwiches, pumpkin pasties, turkey legs… (We had to cut him off. This interview was conducted before his death. RIP, I suppose.)

Gregory Goyle

I’m not sure. I know I won’t miss homework. My father had to bribe my professors so I could pass my classes. (He regretted it as soon as he said it.)
